About Us

Where the City Meets the Sky

Perched atop Hotel Kingship by Kahlon, Cilantrro is Lucknow's finest rooftop dining destination. We believe that great food is not just about ingredients — it's about the air you breathe, the lights you see, and the company you keep.

Our culinary team draws from the rich tradition of Awadhi cooking while embracing global influences — offering a menu that spans the tandoor, the wok, and the wood-fired oven, all under one open sky.
